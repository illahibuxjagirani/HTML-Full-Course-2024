
HTML Lists:

1. Ordered Lists:
<ol>
used when sequence of items matters
==> items are automatically numbered
Syntax:
<ol>
  <li>Januaray</li>
  <li>Feburary</li>
  <li>March</li>
  <li>April</li>
</ol>



you can customize numbering style:
<ol type="1"> for numbers (default)
<ol type="A"> for uppercase letters
<ol type="a"> for lowercase letters
<ol type="I"> for uppercase Roman numerals
<ol type="i"> for lowercase Roman numerals









2. Unordered Lists:
<ul>
==> used when order of items does not matters
==> items are displayed with bullet points(can be changed)

Customizing Unordered Lists:
<ul type="disc"> for filled circles (default)
<ul type="circle"> for hollow circles
<ul type="square"> for squares
